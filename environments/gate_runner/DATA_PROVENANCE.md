# ECB FX data provenance

Gate Runner bundles one pinned source snapshot for its optional `ecb_fx`
profile.

## Source

- **Publisher:** European Central Bank
- **Dataset:** EXR, daily euro foreign exchange reference rates
- **Coverage:** 29 currencies, 2009-01-01 through 2024-12-31 in the source
  query; 4,098 common observed dates from 2009-01-02 through 2024-12-31 after
  holiday rows without values are excluded at runtime
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-10
- **API query:**
  `https://data-api.ecb.europa.eu/service/data/EXR/D.AUD+BGN+BRL+CAD+CHF+CNY+CZK+DKK+GBP+HKD+HUF+IDR+ILS+INR+JPY+KRW+MXN+MYR+NOK+NZD+PHP+PLN+RON+SEK+SGD+THB+TRY+USD+ZAR.EUR.SP00.A?startPeriod=2009-01-01&endPeriod=2024-12-31&format=csvdata`
- **Source CSV SHA-256:**
  `c95c69519eb159bff0287f33cde600d065876bc401103c542b1da439d88b4756`
- **Bundled gzip SHA-256:**
  `2e39da0d83bfbdb6d0575b1e481f2094ea974018b33473785d9349e2d04d7f3f`

Source: ECB statistics.

## Reuse terms

The ECB states that publicly available ESCB statistics may be reused free of
charge when the source is quoted and the statistics are reproduced accurately.
The bundled gzip decompresses to the exact standard CSV returned by the API;
Gate Runner's Apache-2.0 license does not replace or relicense the ECB data.

- Reuse policy: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/stats/ecb_statistics/governance_and_quality_framework/html/usage_policy.en.html
- Disclaimer and copyright: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/services/using-our-site/disclaimer/html/index.en.html
- API examples: https://data.ecb.europa.eu/help/api/data-examples

## Runtime processing

Gate Runner explicitly performs these transformations after loading the pinned
source export:

1. removes source rows whose `OBS_STATUS` is `H` and whose observation value is
   empty;
2. checks that all remaining observations are daily ECB reference rates against
   the euro with final status `A`;
3. requires the same 4,098 dates for all 29 currencies;
4. labels each series `EUR` plus its source currency code, such as `EURUSD`; and
5. attaches a constant 5 bps spread proxy generated by Gate Runner. The spread
   proxy is not ECB data.

The source observation values are not inverted, adjusted, interpolated, or
otherwise changed.

## Limitations

ECB reference rates are published for information, not as executable dealing
quotes. They provide close-like daily observations but no open, high, low,
volume, or transaction-cost data. Gate Runner results are benchmark scores, not
evidence of realizable trading performance or investment advice.
